About

Our goal with this website is to provide a space for everyone who wants to to voice their ideas. We set clear guidelines for what can and can't be said in pieces, and never censor for an opposing viewpoint.

Open discussion is imperative to continue the pursuit of knowledge, so please remain open minded about things you read anywhere, and feel free to contact us directly with any questions or opinions.

We hope this can be a platform for anyone to share an idea with the world, please enjoy.
